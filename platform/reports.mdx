Laminar sends periodic summary reports to your email so you can keep track of [signal](/signals) activity across all projects in a workspace without opening the dashboard.

## Default subscriptions

When you create your first workspace, Laminar automatically subscribes you to two reports:

- **Weekdays signals summary** — Delivered on weekdays (Monday through Friday) summarizing signal events from the previous day.
- **Weekly signals summary** — Delivered once a week (Sunday) summarizing signal events from the previous week.

Both reports cover all projects in the workspace and are sent to the email address associated with your account.

## Manage report subscriptions

1. Go to your **Workspace** page and select **Reports** from the sidebar.
2. You will see the list of available reports with their delivery schedule.
3. Use the **toggle switch** next to each report to subscribe or unsubscribe.

When subscribed, the report card shows your email address and a **Subscribed** badge. When you unsubscribe, you will stop receiving that report until you re-enable it.

## What is included in a report

Each report summarizes signal events across every project in the workspace for the relevant time period. This gives you a quick overview of detected patterns, recurring issues, and notable activity without needing to check each project individually.
