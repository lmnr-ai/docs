Laminar is OpenTelemetry-native. Every integration on this page plugs into the same pipeline: `Laminar.initialize()` (or a framework-specific exporter) wires your app into Laminar, and every LLM call, tool call, and sub-agent turn lands in the [transcript view](/platform/viewing-traces), is queryable from [SQL](/platform/sql-editor), and can be matched against [Signals](/signals/introduction).

Most integrations are auto-instrumented. Install the Laminar SDK, set `LMNR_PROJECT_API_KEY`, and call `Laminar.initialize()` once at the top of your app. Your existing code keeps working.

## Don't see your framework?

Anything that emits OpenTelemetry spans works with Laminar. Trace your own functions with [`observe`](/tracing/structure/observe-decorator), create [manual spans](/tracing/structure/manual-span-creation), or point an existing OTel exporter at the [Laminar OTLP endpoint](/tracing/otel).

If you'd like us to auto-instrument a framework we don't cover yet, [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/lmnr-ai/lmnr/issues) or ping us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/nNFUUDAKub).
